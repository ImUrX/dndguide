Dungeons & Dragons

Enter a World of Magic

Dungeons and Dragons tells the tale of the mightiest of heroes: powerful magic-wielders, mighty warlords, lurking assassins and witches with eldritch power, facing the most horrific kinds of supernatural and fantastical beasts, enemies, and even gods of the deep. The only life worth living is the one filled with peril, dignity, riches, or the heat of battle! Smash heads open with your warhammer, backstab important nobles form the shadows or char black the earth of the battlefield with fireballs. Visit otherwordly dimensions, domains of the gods, and higher planes of existance, discover the secrets of magic and the divine entities around it, unlock the secret to immortality or become an unparalleled existance.
What will you be? A bloodlust-crazed barbarian? A sneaky and subtle rogue? An eldritch and demonic warlock? A faithful and dutiful paladin? A figther, lustful for the sound of clashing steel? The eyes and ears of the gods, a cleric? A sorcerer, a being born from magic? A nature-bending druid, or a secret-keeping ranger?
The one that guides, shapes, and models this tale, is you. Do you dare?
